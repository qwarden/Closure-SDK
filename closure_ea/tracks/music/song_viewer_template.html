This view is laid out by containment and order first, not by free geometry. The angle shows where something sits in the song. The color intensity and labels still come from the quaternion data.

View

This is an onion view, not a star map. The outer ring is the raw stream. Bars wrap that stream. Segments wrap the bars. The piece is the center identity that holds them all.

Interpretation

The adapter gives the cuts. The lattice gives the identities. The raw ring shows time. The bar ring shows emitted bar objects. The segment ring shows the higher closures. The center is the whole-piece identity.
